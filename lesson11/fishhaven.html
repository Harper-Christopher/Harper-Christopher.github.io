Fish Haven, best fishing this side of the Mississippi.
By Christopher Harper

Sitting right along the edge of bear like, Fish Haven is one of the best locations in the West for fishing. With Bonneville whitefish, Bear Lake sculpin, Bonneville cisco, and Bear Lake whitefish, you could spend hours upon hours sitting along the lake shore or cruising around the lake in a boat fishing the day away.

With this 109 square mile freshwater lake, you could spend endless weekends on the shore or in the lake during our beautiful summers with temps that usual sit around a comfy 80's degrees all summer long. There are great opportunities to pack up and leave all the busy city life behind, with plenty of camp ground, hiking trails, and plenty of things to do, you'll want to make Fish Haven your home. Winter time is no different, when the temperature dips below freezing and the lake starts to turn to ice, bundle up with a fresh cup of hot chocolate, and enjoy sitting around ice fishing where the fish are known to be very active. Then come enjoy our yearly event of Light the Tree.
