Octoberfest in downtown Soda Springs.
By Christopher Harper

Octoberfest within beautiful Soda Springs has turned a small little town in Idaho to one that is now known nation wide. From the beautiful rolling hills to the busy town life, you won't get bored being here taking it all in.

Octoberfest has definitely put Soda Springs on the map. Every year in October, this little town of only 3,058 people explodes and comes to life with that number more then tripling with people from all over the nation coming from near and far to enjoy this once in a life time event.
