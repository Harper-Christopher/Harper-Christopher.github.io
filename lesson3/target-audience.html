The target audience of weatherstationcreation.com web site is described below:

  • Who: Farmers to Truck Drivers
  • Ages: 20's - 80's
  • Motivatoin: To see what the weather will be for a specific day, hour, or week.

Personas

John Doe

  • Occupation: Farmer
  • Demographics and Education: Agricultural Science Degree
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: Uses the site to see what their crops needs will be.
  • Social:
  • Technology: Uses his desktop computer and tablet to view most web sites.

Mack Joe

  • Occupation: Truck Driver
  • Demographics and Education: High School Diploma.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: Uses the site while he's on the road to see what the weather will be like while driving through.
  • Social:
  • Technology: Mainly uses his Iphone 11 as he's on the road most days.

Scenarios

  1. John looks through the web site in order to see if there is rain in the forcast to see if he needs to use his sprinklers for his crops.
  2. John looks through the web site in order to see if there is going to be a hard freeze so he can protect his crops.
  3. John looks through the web site to see if any storms are coming during the week so he can plan his harvset.
  4. Mack looks through the web site to see if the weather will be snowy as he drives through.
  5. Mack looks through the web site to see what time storms will roll through so he can plan his travels.
  6. Mack looks through the web site to see if the weather will be below freezing so he knows if the roads will be icy.
