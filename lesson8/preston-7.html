Preston, Idaho named number one town to live in 2019.
By Christopher Harper

Preston Idaho has been named number one town in America to live in. When it comes to towns that seem to have it all, Preston Idaho has everything that one could look for, to the endless things to do, to the vast career opportunities this is one location that you don't want to miss out on.

Here you'll find endless recreational areas to 4 wheel, whether you're into dirt bikes, ATV's, or big lifted trucks. We have the perfect spot for you. You'll also find plenty of places to hike, camp, and spend all weekend in nature. There are also plenty of areas to go out to fancy dinners with the many 5 star restaurants right at your fingers top within a 10 mile radius. After experiencing some of the amazing restaurants we have to offer, check out all the movie locations with the newest top movies, go bowling, mini golfing, and anything else you find exciting and fun to do.
